Healthy

If you're running a childcare center and want to serve healthy beverages to your kids every day, premium quality 100% fruit juice concentrates from Penny Juice are for you.

All of our juice concentrates are made from 100% juice. They do not contain any artificial colors and are sugar-free.

The ingredients in our concentrates are concentrated fruit juice, filtered water, malic acid, ascorbic acid (vitamin C), natural and artificial flavors, and caramel. Sodium benzoate is added to maintain freshness.
